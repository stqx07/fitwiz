Welcome to the Potion Shop

Purchase powerful potions to unleash damage on your foes or restore your health during intense duels.

Cure for Boils

A potion which removes boils, such as those produced by the Pimple Jinx.

Heal: 40 Health

Cost: 64 skillpoints

Wiggenweld Potion

A healing potion with the power to sterilise and heal minor injuries, and was the antidote to the Sleeping Draught and the Draught of Living Death.

Heal: 50 Health

Cost: 80 skillpoints

Essence of Dittany

A magical solution that possesses powerful curative properties, which could be used on open shallow wounds for immediate healing and skin regeneration.

Heal: 75 Health

Cost: 120 skillpoints

Dizziness Draught

A potion that provokes a sensation of lightheadedness and loss of balance on the drinker.

Damage: 20 Damage

Cost: 32 skillpoints

Confusion Concoction

A potion which causes confusion in the drinker.

Damage: 30 Damage

Cost: 48 skillpoints

Weakness Potion

A potion whose fumes induced weakness, both physically and mentally.

Damage: 50 Damage

Cost: 80 skillpoints
