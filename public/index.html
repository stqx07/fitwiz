About the Application

This application is designed to gamify your fitness journey with a magical touch! Explore various challenges and enhance your experience by earning skillpoints and rewards. Step into a magical world where you can duel against mythical creatures to test your skills and earn rewards. Buy magical items like wands, potions, and spells to enhance your abilities. Get sorted into your Hogwarts house and work together with housemates to climb the leaderboard. Keep track of your progress by viewing your activity history, and see how you rank against others in individual and house leaderboards. The adventure is yours to create!

Home: Return to the main page to explore what the application offers. You can also check out the leaderboard to see how users and houses are ranking!

Fitness Challenges: Participate in exciting fitness challenges to earn skillpoints and improve your fitness journey.

Diagon Alley: Buy magical items to aid in your adventures.
  • Wand Shop: Purchase wands to boost your dueling abilities.
  • Potion Shop: Stock up on potions to restore health and enhance abilities.
  • Spell Shop: Learn spells to gain an edge in duels.

Vault: View and manage the items you've purchased, including wands, potions, and spells.

Duelling Club: Test your skills in duels against magical creatures.

Profile: View your personal information, edit your username, check your Hogwarts house (or choose one if you haven't been sorted yet), and review your activity history.

Login/Register/Logout: Access your account or create a new one to start your magical journey. Once logged in, you can easily log out to secure your account when you're done exploring.
